Shipping Policy

1. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes we offer Free shipping depending on the Promo per month. Our regular shipping (COD) is Php 99 for 1 to 3 pcs. However if you wish to expedite your order within metro manila, an additional 150 or 200 will be added to your final bill. This is considered as a special request. Please let us know if you'd like to expedite your order by sending us an email at tipidityph2021@gmail.com.

2. Who is your delivery service?

We have J&T Express as our courier depending on your location, our courier might call you within the day of delivery so please make sure to have your registered mobile phone nearby in case they call. Our delivery service cannot promise a time period since they follow a route sheet within your area. Please bear with us.

3. How many times will they deliver my product?

Our delivery partner will attempt 1-2x before considering your order as cancelled. Once failed, your address and name may be included in the blacklist of COD and you will be posted and banned in all our sites and pages.

4. How do I track my order?

Please email us at tipidityph2021@gmail.com for your order updates, provinces and remote areas especially in Mindanao can take more than 10 days. You could also track your order using the tracking number sent to you via sms.

5. I was delivered the wrong item.

Please email us asap at tipidityph2021@gmail.com for immediate action, rest assured we will do our best to correct this mistake.

6. How many days should I receive my order?

Cash on Delivery

  • Provinces : 3-10 Business Days
